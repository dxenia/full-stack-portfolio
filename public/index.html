About Me

Who am I?

Hello there! My name is Dana, I originally come from Turin, a city located by the Italian Alps, although I currently reside in Malmö with my boyfriend and cat. In my personal life I love drawing, knitting, and reading. My favourite genres are poetry and self-improvement. I like to spend time in the nature, baking and cooking - Italian food is at the top of my list.

My studies were always characterized by a holistic and immersive nature. I took care of business development operations for several companies, within tech (KPaaS), e-commerce and marketing. I also have a few years of experience working in hospitality, becoming a waitress during my student years to support my own self, as well as within podcast editing.
